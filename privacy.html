Privacy Policy for Zerox RP Bot

Last updated: 05/12/2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy outlines how Zerox RP Bot ("the Bot") collects, uses, and protects your information when used within Discord servers.

2. Data Collected

  • Server IDs – Used to store settings and configurations per server.
  • User IDs – Used to log command usage and customize responses.
  • Command Usage Data – Tracked to improve bot functionality and performance.

No personal, sensitive, or identifiable information (e.g., IP addresses, email addresses, etc.) is collected.

3. Data Usage

The data collected is used solely to operate and improve the functionality of Zerox RP Bot. It is never sold or shared with third parties.

4. Data Storage

Data is stored securely and only as long as necessary to provide the Bot's features. Users may request the removal of their server's data at any time.

5. Data Sharing

We do not share any user or server data with third parties unless required by law or to comply with legal obligations.

6. Children's Privacy

Zerox RP Bot is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 13. If you are under 13, you may not use the Bot.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the Bot indicates acceptance of any changes.
